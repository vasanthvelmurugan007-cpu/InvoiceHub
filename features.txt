InvoiceHub Billing Software - Features & Highlights

Core Billing & Inventory
* You can create professional invoices quickly with automatic tax and total calculations.
* The system keeps track of your products and alerts you when stock is running low.
* You can manage all your customer details, including their GSTIN and contact information, in one place.
* The dashboard gives you a real-time snapshot of your sales, expenses, unpaid invoices, and overall profit.
* You can easily share invoices directly with customers via WhatsApp or Email with a single click.

Premium Features
* You can create Estimates (Proforma Invoices) to send quotes to your clients before billing them.
* You can generate Purchase Orders to manage the stock you buy from your vendors.
* You can issue Delivery Challans when transporting goods to customers.
* The software allows you to track all your business expenses to understand your cash flow better.
* You can manage your vendor database just like your customers.

Compliance & Reporting (Premium)
* The system automatically generates GSTR-1 ready reports for your sales.
* You can view a complete tax summary (GSTR-3B logic) showing your input and output tax.
* You get detailed Profit & Loss reports to track your business performance over time.

Customization & Branding
* You can personalize your bills by uploading your company logo and digital signature.
* You can add your bank account details, UPI ID, and custom terms & conditions to every invoice.
* The system automatically converts your total bill amounts into words (e.g., "Five Thousand Only").

Security & Reliability
* Every user gets their own private, secure workspace where their data is kept completely separate from others.
* You can enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) to add an extra layer of security to your account.
* You can download complete backups of your database at any time to keep your data safe.
* The system supports an Admin dashboard to manage user accounts and approve premium subscriptions.
